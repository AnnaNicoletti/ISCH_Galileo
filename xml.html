About the TEI Encoding

As part of the LODLAM project on Galileo Galilei, this section presents a digital edition of the Dialogo sopra i due massimi sistemi del mondo, encoded in XML following the standards of the Text Encoding Initiative (TEI). The edition focuses on semantic markup of the text's structure, characters, dialogue sections, bibliographic metadata, and editorial provenance.

The TEI file includes:

  • A full <teiHeader> with bibliographic and editorial metadata
  • Encoding of front matter and editorial notes
  • Detailed markup of dialogues with participants
  • Named entities and measurement units
